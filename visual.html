The point of this game is to clear all levels, collect items and train a monster.

How to do it?
Simply use the arrow keys for navigation and certain keys to choose between options .

You will meet a bunch of different creatures along your way. Some will love to help you while you'll have to fight others. The same applies to the characters which are posted along your way in the levels.

Your monster has a certain strength which is depends of its health points (HP). Has your monster reached a certain amount of HPs it will level up.
