Monsters is a simple game to spend your time on. --different
Catch different monsters and train them while discovering multiple free area levels!
Navigation: Arrow Keys Fight Options:
  • (a)ttack
  • (c)atch
  • (f)eed
  • (r)un away
    • Use numbers to change between caught animals. Collect: -item img - to improve your monsters health -waterstone - to walk on water -key - to get into the next level With each won fight your monster gets stronger and eventually levels up. -LIST OF ITEMS You can take care of multiple monsters at once and choose a favourite which will be on your side in a fight. Switch between your favourites with the number indicated on their left side at the bottom of the screen. The number on the right side of their picture shows their current level. --picture of that
    But watch out. If you are involved in a fight with a wild monster you have the chance of catching it while another trainer will simply knock you out!
