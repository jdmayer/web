Monsters is a visually rich game which enables the player to catch different species of monsters and train them while discovering multiple free area levels!

Same principle as always, catch, feed, beat! You can train multiple monsters at once but only on of each race.
 goodies  strenghten your monster during a trainer fight ('feed')
 waterstone  play Jesus and walk over water
 key  find it to unlock the next level
 favourite monster  your fighting compagnon
 monster index  press this number while not in a fight to change fighting monster
 level of the monster

You will meet other trainers, who you can only battle with a monster on your side. But some of them are good hearted and just want to chat.
