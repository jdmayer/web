The point of this game is to clear all levels and to beat the bad guys.

How to do it?
Simply use the arrow keys for navigation, 'enter' to choose between options and 'm' to open the menue.

You will meet a bunch of different creatures along your way. Some will love to help you while you'll have to fight others.

Good luck on your way!
